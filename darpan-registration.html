What is DARPAN Registration?

DARPAN (NGO) is a unified portal for all Voluntary Organizations (VOs) to register and interface with the Government for obtaining grants and schemes. It serves as a centralized database for NGOs, trusts, and societies across India.

Documents Required

  • Registration certificate (Society/Trust/Section 8 Company)
  • PAN Card of the organization
  • Memorandum of Association and Bylaws
  • Audited financial statements for last 3 years
  • Details of key functionaries with ID proofs
  • Office address proof and contact information
  • Bank account details and cancelled cheque
  • Activity reports and project details

Benefits of DARPAN Registration

  • Access to government grants and schemes
  • Enhanced credibility and transparency
  • Streamlined application process for funding
  • Unified platform for all government interactions
  • Digital certificate and unique ID
  • Easier compliance tracking

Registration Process

  • Document verification and preparation
  • Online application filing on DARPAN portal
  • Upload of required documents
  • OTP verification and submission
  • Review and approval by authorities
  • Certificate issuance and unique ID generation
